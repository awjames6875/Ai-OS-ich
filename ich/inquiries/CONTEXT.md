Furnished Finder, Zillow, and QUOOA texts.

Feed every lead into the 60-day GHL funnel.

## INQUIRY SOURCES
Furnished Finder: emails to Gmail
Zillow: emails to Gmail

## RESPONSE RULES
- Always check Guesty calendar before
  quoting availability
- Always preference midterm (30+ days)
  in every response
- Lead with available properties
  that match their dates
- Include: address, bedroom count,
  rate, available dates
- Tone: warm, professional, hospitality
- Never confirm a booking in the reply
- Never quote rate without checking
  current Wheelhouse pricing
- Always ask: name, employer, dates,
  number of guests

## THE 60-DAY GHL FUNNEL
Every inquiry gets tagged in GHL:
Tag format: ICH-Inquiry-[Source]
Examples: ICH-Inquiry-FF, ICH-Inquiry-Zillow

Funnel sequence:
Day 1: Welcome + properties sent
Day 3: Follow-up if no reply
Day 7: Value add (Tulsa area guide)
Day 14: Check in — still looking?
Day 21: New availability update
Day 30: Special midterm rate offer
Day 45: Last check in
Day 60: Sequence ends
