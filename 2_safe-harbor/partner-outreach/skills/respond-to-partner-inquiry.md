# Skill: Respond to Partner Inquiry
# Room: /2_safe-harbor/partner-outreach/
# Trigger: A daycare, after-school program, or similar partner replies to outreach

## LANGUAGE RULES — CHECK BEFORE WRITING A SINGLE WORD
NEVER USE: therapy, therapist, mental health treatment, mental health counseling
ALWAYS USE: emotional wellness, support sessions, licensed counselor, Body and Brain program
B2B RULE: Never mention dollar amounts — say "new revenue stream" or "facility fee partnership"

## STEP 1 — READ THE REPLY
Identify what they're asking:
- Are they interested? → use INTERESTED template
- Do they have questions? → answer using the offer language below
- Are they declining? → use DECLINE template (do not push)

## INTERESTED TEMPLATE

---

Hi [First Name],

So glad to hear from you! The Body and Brain program is a great fit for
facilities like yours that already care deeply about the kids they serve.

Here's the short version of how it works:

You provide the space — we bring the licensed counselor.
Your families get access to emotional wellness support sessions for their kids.
You add a new revenue stream with zero cost to your facility.

The next step is just a quick 15-minute call to see if it's a good fit.
Are you free this week? I can work around your schedule.

Warm regards,
Adam James
Safe Harbor Behavioral Health
ajames@safeharborbehavioralhealth.com

---

## QUESTIONS TEMPLATE
If they ask: "What exactly is this?"

---

Great question. The Body and Brain program pairs a licensed counselor
with your existing facility to provide emotional wellness support sessions
for children. Parents opt in — it's an add-on to what you already offer.

No additional staff, no cost to your facility. You simply provide the space
and receive a facility fee partnership in return.

Happy to walk you through it on a quick call — does [Day] work for you?

---

## DECLINE TEMPLATE
If they're not interested:

---

Totally understand — appreciate you taking the time to respond.
If circumstances change or you'd like to revisit in the future, we're here.

Wishing you and your team all the best.

Adam James
Safe Harbor Behavioral Health
